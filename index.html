Bienvenue à French Journey

Your comprehensive guide to mastering Canadian French (Quebec), from A1 to B2.

Beginner

A1 Level

Kickstart your journey with the absolute fundamentals. Perfect for complete beginners.

  • Alphabet, phonetics & liaison rules
  • Greetings, family & professions
  • Key verbs (Être, Avoir, -ER/-IR/-RE)
  • Daily routines & basic past tense
 Elementary

A2 Level

Expand your survival skills to form complex sentences and navigate daily life.

  • Imparfait vs Passé Composé
  • Travel, housing & health vocabulary
  • Object pronouns (le, la, lui, leur)
  • Real-life scenarios (shopping, doctor)
 Intermediate

B1 Level

Become an independent speaker capable of reasoning and expressing opinions.

  • The Subjunctive & Conditional moods
  • Expressing opinions on society & media
  • Quebec history, politics & arts
  • Handling complaints and conflicts
 Upper Intermediate

B2 Level

Master nuanced French, slang, and prepare for academic or professional environments.

  • Advanced discourse markers & passive voice
  • Business, Law, Science & Higher Ed
  • Quebec identity, humour & Joual (slang)
  • TEFAQ/DELF B2 exam preparation
